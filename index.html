Our Story

Swiftsync Manage Services is a leading technology services company dedicated to providing innovative cloud-based management solutions for small and medium-sized businesses (SMBs). Our mission is to empower SMBs with cutting-edge technology that streamlines operations, boosts productivity, and drives growth. At Swiftsync, we believe in the power of technology to transform businesses. Our vision is to become the most trusted partner for SMBs seeking to leverage cloud-based solutions for their management needs. We are guided by our core values of innovation, integrity, customer-centricity, and continuous improvement. Our comprehensive suite of cloud-based management tools addresses key challenges faced by SMBs, including resource allocation, project management, customer relationship management, and financial planning. By offering intuitive, scalable, and cost-effective solutions, we help our clients focus on what matters most growing their business.

Company History

Founded in 2015 by a team of technology enthusiasts and business management experts, Swiftsync Manage Services has rapidly grown to become a respected name in the cloud-based management solutions industry. Our journey began with a simple idea: to make enterprise-level management tools accessible and affordable for SMBs

Key milestones in our Growth Include:

  • 2015: Company founded and launch of our first cloud-based project management tool
  • 2017: Introduction of our integrated CRM and financial planning modules
  • 2019: Expansion of services to cover the entire APAC region
  • 2021: Recognition as "Most Innovative Cloud Solution Provider" by TechXcellence Awards
  • 2023: Surpassed 10,000 active business clients globally
